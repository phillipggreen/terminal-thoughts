### 1. Breakpoints

### 2. Step Through Code

- **Step Into**: Dive into functions

- **Type** of error (e.g., `TypeError`, `SyntaxError`)
- **Line number** where it occurred
- **Message** describing what went wrong

### 6. Divide and Conquer
